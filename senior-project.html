As a current senior at Bard College, I am working on my senior thesis project, commonly known as the "Sproj."
For photography majors like myself, this project consists of creating a series of photographs
to be hung as a gallery show this spring. The following images are a selection of large format
black and white film photos I have made so far this school year. This is still very much a work-in-progress
but I am excited to share where I am at with this project now.
